About Us

Cold Noses is a non-profit organization based in Ireland dedicated to providing loving homes for cats in need. We collaborate with various organizations and rescue cats from the streets, ensuring they find a family to call their own.

Fun Facts About Cats


Cats and Mental Health

Interacting with cats can lower blood pressure and reduce anxiety, contributing positively to mental health.

How Many Cats Do You Need to Be Happy?

Studies show that owning just one cat can significantly boost your happiness and reduce stress levels. Imagine what two can do!
