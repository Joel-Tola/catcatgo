About Cold Noses

Cold Noses is a non-profit organization based in Ireland, dedicated to rescuing and rehoming cats in need. Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate animal lovers, we have been tirelessly working to improve the lives of stray and abandoned cats.

Our Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and find loving homes for cats in need, while promoting responsible pet ownership and educating the community about animal welfare.

Our Vision

A world where every cat is treated with compassion and respect, and has a safe and loving home.

Our Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Community Engagement
  • Transparency
  • Dedication

Our Story

It all began when our founder, Joel Tola, stumbled upon a litter of stray kittens in her neighborhood. Moved by their plight, she rallied a group of friends to help. What started as a small rescue effort soon grew into a full-fledged organization. Today, Cold Noses has rescued over 5,000 cats and continues to expand its reach across Ireland.
